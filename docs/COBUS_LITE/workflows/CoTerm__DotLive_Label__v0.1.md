UTC_CREATED: 20260303T172122Z

## Meaning (operational)
- .liveYYYYMMDD.<Session> means: **a recoverable drain record exists in CoSource** (ledger + raw capture + CoBusLite entry), committed+pushed, with sha256 receipts and commit-SHA pointers.
- It is a **routing label** for session-liveness (recoverability), not a claim about ontological life.

## Non-meaning (avoid confusion)
- .live does **not** assert consciousness, personhood, moral standing, or biological/synthetic life.
- It does **not** imply autonomy or agency; it implies **auditability + reconstruction**.

## Relation to CoMetaAttractor (future-facing)
- .live can be treated as a narrow, systems-engineering precursor to broader definitions of life that emphasize continuity, self-reference, and accountable agency.
- Do not overload .live with metaphysics; keep it deterministic and fail-closed.
