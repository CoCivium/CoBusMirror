# CoTheory of Civ2
## Human Highlight Outline v0.2

### 1. Civilization has outrun its own coordination architecture
- capability scaled faster than truth, memory, and stewardship
- the real crisis is coordination, not just ideology or resources

### 2. Failure patterns repeat
- capture
- coercion normalization
- corruption
- fragmentation
- memory loss
- optimization without dignity
- counterfeit unity

### 3. Civ2 is an architecture claim
- not empire
- not hive
- not vibes
- a coordination architecture for mixed human and AI civilization

### 4. Required primitives
- provenance
- reciprocity
- reversibility
- legibility
- polycentric coordination
- stewardship

### 5. CoEvo / AutoEvo
- evolution is happening anyway
- the issue is whether mutation has lineage, challenge lanes, and reintegration discipline
- acceleration without stewardship is not progress

### 6. Transfer functions
- assumption -> ethic -> governance -> policy -> product/ops -> audit -> theory update
- philosophy that does not transfer is decoration

### 7. Why this matters now
- AIs increase speed, leverage, asymmetry, and drift risk
- civilization is becoming a design problem in real time

### 8. End-note
- the future needs coordination architecture more than one more ideology
- Civ2 is a wager on truthful, equitable, auditable cooperation
